Real Homemade Bagels

A delicious bagel recipe for that authentic bagel flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 1¼ cups water
  • 4½ cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon instant yeast
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1 cup honey (Optional)
